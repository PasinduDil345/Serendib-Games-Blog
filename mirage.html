Assassins Creed Mirage

Assassin's Creed Mirage is an upcoming action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft. Set in 9th-century Baghdad during the Islamic Golden Age, the game follows Basim Ibn Ishaq, a character introduced in Assassin's Creed Valhalla. Basim’s story is set before his time in Valhalla, exploring his early days as a member of the Assassin Brotherhood, uncovering the secrets of his origins, and seeking vengeance for past wrongs. The game will return to the franchise’s roots, offering a more compact, narrative-driven experience compared to recent Assassin's Creed entries like Odyssey and Valhalla. It will feature stealth-based gameplay, parkour mechanics, and the traditional blend of historical exploration and action.

    Minimum Requirements

    • CPU: Intel Core i3-7100 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200
    • RAM: 8 GB
    • Video Card: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon RX 5500 XT

    • Dedicated Video RAM: 6144 MB
    • Pixel Shader: 6.0

    • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
    • Free Disk Space: 75 GB
